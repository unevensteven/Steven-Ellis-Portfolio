About me.

An experienced engineer a background in a range of engineering disciplines. I have a passion for technology and how it can be used to enhance our lives, and an interest in web development and software engineering. I thrive on the challenges that come with building systems from the ground up, or improving their efficiency or user experience. I am dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and industry best practices.
In my career, I have had the opportunity to travel extensively, broadening my horizons and gaining valuable insights into different cultures and perspectives. These experiences have shaped my ability to adapt, think creatively, and approach problem-solving with an open mind. I lived in Asia for several years, and learned to speak another language, allowing me to apreciate cultures diverse from my own. I love to scuba dive during my time off, have dived all around asia and pacific region, and I am a certified scuba instructor.
I started the SCS-Coalition scheme in 2023, and this webpage serves as a platform to showcase my skills, projects, and achievements. Feel free to explore and learn more about my work. I am always eager to collaborate on exciting ventures and contribute to innovative solutions.
Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you!
